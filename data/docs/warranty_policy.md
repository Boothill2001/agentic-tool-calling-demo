# Warranty Policy

## Coverage

All physical products sold through our platform come with a **12-month limited warranty** from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects and malfunctions under normal use conditions.

## What Is Covered

- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
- Electronic component failures under normal operating conditions
- Structural defects that appear during the warranty period

## What Is NOT Covered

- Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or unauthorized modifications
- Normal wear and tear, including battery degradation
- Water damage (unless the product is rated as waterproof)
- Cosmetic damage such as scratches or dents
- Products purchased from unauthorized resellers

## Claim Process

1. Contact customer support with your order ID and description of the issue
2. Provide photos or videos demonstrating the defect
3. Our team will review and respond within 48 hours
4. If approved, you will receive a prepaid shipping label for the defective item
5. Choose between repair, replacement, or refund (at our discretion based on availability)

## Extended Warranty

Platinum members automatically receive an **extended 24-month warranty** on all purchases. Gold members can purchase extended warranty for an additional 10% of the product price at checkout.
