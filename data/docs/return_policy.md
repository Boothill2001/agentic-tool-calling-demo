# Return Policy

## Return Window

Customers have **14 days** from the delivery date to initiate a return. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and include all accessories and documentation.

## Restocking Fee

A **10% restocking fee** applies to all returns unless the item is defective or was shipped incorrectly. Platinum members are exempt from the restocking fee.

## Non-Returnable Items

- Perishable goods and food items
- Personal care and hygiene products (opened)
- Downloadable software and digital content
- Gift cards and prepaid cards
- Items marked as "Final Sale"

## Return Process

1. Log into your account and navigate to Order History
2. Select the order and click "Request Return"
3. Choose the reason for return and submit
4. Print the prepaid return label (defective/incorrect items only)
5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping point
6. Refund or exchange is processed within 5-7 business days after inspection

## Exchanges

Exchanges are available for the same item in a different size/color. If the replacement item is a different price, the difference will be charged or refunded accordingly.
