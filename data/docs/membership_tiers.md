# Membership Tiers

## Overview

Our loyalty program has three tiers based on annual spending:

| Tier | Annual Spending Threshold | Benefits |
|------|--------------------------|----------|
| Standard | Default (all customers) | Basic support, standard shipping rates |
| Gold | 10,000,000 VND+ | Free standard shipping, 5% discount, priority support |
| Platinum | 30,000,000 VND+ | Free all shipping, 10% discount, 24-month warranty, dedicated account manager |

## Gold Benefits

- Free standard shipping on all orders
- 5% discount on all purchases (applied automatically)
- Priority customer support (response within 4 hours)
- Early access to sales and new product launches
- Birthday bonus: 500,000 VND credit

## Platinum Benefits

- Free shipping on all tiers (standard, express, same-day)
- 10% discount on all purchases
- Extended 24-month warranty on all products
- Dedicated account manager
- Priority customer support (response within 1 hour)
- Exclusive access to limited edition products
- Birthday bonus: 1,000,000 VND credit
- No restocking fee on returns
- Expedited refund processing (2-3 business days)

## Tier Evaluation

Tiers are evaluated annually based on the previous 12 months of spending. Customers are notified 30 days before a potential tier change. Downgrade protection: if spending falls below threshold, customers retain their current tier for one additional quarter.
