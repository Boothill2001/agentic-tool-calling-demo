# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Tiers

| Tier | Delivery Time | Cost | Minimum Order |
|------|--------------|------|---------------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | 30,000 VND | No minimum |
| Express | 2-3 business days | 65,000 VND | No minimum |
| Same-day | Within 24 hours | 120,000 VND | HCMC/Hanoi only |

## Free Shipping

Free standard shipping is available for orders over **1,000,000 VND**. Gold and Platinum members receive free standard shipping on all orders regardless of amount.

## Tracking

All shipments include tracking information sent via email and SMS. Customers can track their orders through the customer portal or by contacting support with their order ID.

## International Shipping

Currently available for Southeast Asian countries only (Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, Malaysia). International orders typically take 7-14 business days and shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

## Delivery Issues

If a package is not delivered within the estimated timeframe plus 3 business days, customers should contact support. We will initiate an investigation with the carrier and provide a replacement or full refund if the package is confirmed lost.
