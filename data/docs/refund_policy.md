# Refund Policy

## Eligibility

Customers may request a full refund within **30 days** of purchase for any reason. After 30 days but within 60 days, a partial refund of 70% of the purchase price may be issued at management discretion.

## Conditions

- The product must be in its original condition with all packaging and accessories
- Digital products and services that have been activated are non-refundable
- Custom or personalized items cannot be refunded unless defective
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

## Process

1. Submit a refund request through the customer portal or contact support
2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number within 24 hours
3. Ship the item back using the provided shipping label (free for defective items)
4. Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned item
5. Refund is issued to the original payment method

## Special Cases

- **Platinum members** receive expedited refund processing (2-3 business days)
- **Defective products** are eligible for full refund plus return shipping reimbursement
- **Orders over 5,000,000 VND** require manager approval for refund processing
